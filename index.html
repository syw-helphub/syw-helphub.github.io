What You’ll Need Before You Begin

Before visiting https://syw.accountonline.com, make sure you have:

  • Your Shop Your Way credit card
  • Your card number, CVV, and personal identification details
  • Your registered phone number or email for verification
  • A stable internet connection
  • A Citi online account (or be ready to create one)

Step-by-Step: Log In to Your SYW Account

Here’s how to log in to syw.accountonline.com:

  1. Open your browser and go to: https://syw.accountonline.com
  2. Locate the login section on the page.
  3. Enter your User ID.
  4. Type your Password.
  5. Click Sign On.
  6. If prompted, complete two-step verification using SMS or email.
  7. Once verified, your SYW account dashboard will load.

Your dashboard lets you view payments, statements, rewards, and more.

How to Activate Your SYW Credit Card

If you are activating your Shop Your Way credit card for the first time:

  1. Visit: https://syw.accountonline.com
  2. Click on Register Your Card or Activate Card.
  3. Enter your credit card number, name, CVV, and other details.
  4. Verify your identity.
  5. Create or log in to your Citi/SYW online account.

Your card will be activated and ready for use. Activation completes within minutes.

How to Register for an Online Account

If you haven’t created your online SYW login yet, follow these steps:

  1. Visit syw.accountonline.com.
  2. Click Register for Online Access.
  3. Enter your credit card number.
  4. Provide personal information (name, DOB, SSN last 4 digits).
  5. Create a Username and Password.
  6. Select your security questions.
  7. Complete verification.

You can now log in anytime using your new credentials.

How to Recover a Forgotten User ID or Password

Forgot User ID

  1. Click Retrieve User ID on the login page.
  2. Enter your registered email or phone number.
  3. Complete verification.
  4. Your User ID will be sent to you.

Forgot Password

  1. Click Reset Password.
  2. Enter your User ID.
  3. Verify your identity through OTP.
  4. Set a new password.

You can now log in again.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

If you encounter problems during login, try these solutions:

Invalid Password

Reset your password using the “Reset Password” link.

Account Locked

Wait 24 hours or unlock it through verification.

Browser Issues

  • Clear cache and cookies.
  • Use incognito/private mode.
  • Try another browser.

Verification Code Not Received

  • Check spam folder.
  • Ensure your phone number/email is correct.
  • Request a new code.

If issues continue, contact Citi customer support for help.
